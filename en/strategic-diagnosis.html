Where Founders Get Stuck

Three Traps. One Is Yours.

Every founder lands in one of these. The diagnosis finds yours in 90 minutes.

KNOW-Stuck

The Architect's Curse. You see the whole building. Nobody else can. Vision deck on version 7. Strategy retreats that feel great, then nothing happens. You know exactly what you want. You just can't make it real.

Your ideas die in your head because the strategy is invisible to everyone else.

SHOW-Stuck

The Founder Trap. Beautiful org chart. Documented processes. And still, nothing works unless YOU touch it. You've built a business that runs on your presence, not your systems.

The exhaustion of being the only one who "gets it."

GROW-Stuck

The Scaling Nightmare. Revenue up. Profits down. Team bigger. Quality worse. You're succeeding yourself into a crisis you can see coming but can't stop.

The foundation is cracking under the weight of your own success.

Why consultants didn't fix it: They gave you frameworks. Templates. Best practices. But every framework they gave you built dependency on their thinking, not yours. And when they left, the strategy left with them.
